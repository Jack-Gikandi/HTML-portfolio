The Best Movies According to Jack

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. Fast X

Over many missions and against impossible odds, Dom Toretto
and his family have outsmarted and outdriven every foe in their
path. Now, they must confront the most lethal opponent they've
ever faced. Fueled by revenge, a terrifying threat emerges from
the shadows of the past to shatter Dom's world and destroy
everything -- and everyone -- he loves.

2. Transformers : Rise of the Beasts

Optimus Prime and the Autobots take on their biggest challenge
yet. When a new threat capable of destroying the entire planet
emerges, they must team up with a powerful faction of
Transformers known as the Maximals to save Earth..

3. Hidden Strike

Two former soldiers face nonstop danger as they travel along
one of the most dangerous roads in the world..
